Windows Computer Hard Drive Replacement

Has your computer started taking longer and longer to boot into the Operating System? Or has it stopped booting entirely? Does it take a long time to open files or applications on your PC? It is possible that the hard drive or solid state drive in your computer is starting to fail or has failed already. Replacing the hard disk or solid state drive in your computer can be a great way of extending the life of your PC, without having to pay nearly as much as it would cost to replace it!

Tc Teks can replace the old hard disk drive in your computer, get the Windows operating system up and running, and attempt data recovery to the furthest extent. If your older PC is still running a hard disk we can even upgrade it to a Solid State drive to give it a whole new jolt of speed! Stop in today with your computer if you are unsure if the drive in your computer is failing, we can run a standard diagnostic of the PC by our trained professional technicians to determine if that may be the case or if some other issue may be causing the slow down.
