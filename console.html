What do you need fixed with your game console?

HDMI Port Repair

Has the HDMI or other video port on your game console stopped putting out video? It is possible that a bad HDMI cord may have damaged the port or bent a pin internally. Tc Teks can professionally disassemble and replace your HDMI or other video port, all while keeping the cost a lot less than purchasing a new game console!

Hard Drive Replacement or Upgrade

Is the hard drive in your game console damaged or in need of being upgraded due to a lack of storage? Our hard drive replacement or upgrade service is exactly what you need to expand the storage in your game console or to repair a broken or failing drive! Our technicians will do everything they can to backup the data on your old drive to ensure you don't lose any of those precious saves, all the while upgrading the storage to ensure you have plently of space to game on!

Diagnostic Services

Are you unsure what may be wrong with your game console? If you are experiencing any issue no matter how severe they are feel free to bring your game console in to one of our locations for our trained technicians to perform a diagnostic to determine exactly what may be wrong with your game console! No matter the age or brand of the console, Tc Teks would be glad to service it for you.
